Building Production-Ready AI Systems

December 4, 2024

After years of deploying AI solutions in enterprise environments, I've learned that the gap between a working prototype and a production system is larger than most teams anticipate. Here are some key lessons.

Start with the End in Mind

Before writing any code, understand how the system will be monitored, maintained, and scaled. Production systems need observability built in from day one—not bolted on later.

Data Quality is Everything

The most sophisticated model will fail if fed poor quality data. Invest heavily in data validation, monitoring for drift, and clear data contracts between systems.

Plan for Failure

AI systems can fail in subtle ways that traditional software doesn't. Build graceful degradation paths, set up alerting for model performance metrics, and always have a fallback strategy.

Document Everything

Future you (and your colleagues) will thank you. Document not just the what, but the why—especially for architectural decisions and model choices.
